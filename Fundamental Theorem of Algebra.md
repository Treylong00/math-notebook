
> [!quote] Theorem
> Every non-constant single variable polynomial with complex coefficients has at least one complex root. This includes polynomials with real coefficients since $\mathbb{R} \subset \mathbb{C}$. Equivalently, the field of complex numbers is algebraically closed.
> [Fundamental theorem of algebra - Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fundamental_theorem_of_algebra)
